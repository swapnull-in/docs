Recurso's billing engine is **open source and free to self-host** — every feature, no
license fees, no usage caps. **Recurso Cloud** is managed hosting from **$299/mo** (we
run hosting, scaling, backups, and SSL). **Enterprise** adds dedicated infrastructure,
compliance, and SLAs.

<Card title="See full pricing" icon="arrow-up-right-from-square" href="https://recurso.dev/pricing" horizontal>
  Plan comparison, usage-based pricing, add-ons, and FAQ — on the Recurso site.
</Card>

## At a glance

| | Open Source | Cloud | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Price** | Free forever | $299/mo + usage | Custom |
| **Hosting** | Self-managed | Managed | Managed / on-prem |
| **Support** | Community | Email | Priority + SLA |
| **Best for** | Teams with DevOps who want full control | Shipping billing without managing infra | High volume, compliance, custom needs |

<Info>
  Self-hosting includes **everything** — the advanced capabilities that are paid add-ons
  on Cloud (churn prediction, advanced analytics, accounting sync, multi-currency FX) are
  free in the open-source edition, because you run the infrastructure.
</Info>
