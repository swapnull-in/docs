## Overview

Recurso's core billing engine is **open source and free to self-host**. Run it on your own infrastructure with full access to every feature, no license fees, and no usage limits.

**Recurso Cloud** is a managed hosting option for teams that want to skip infrastructure setup. We handle hosting, scaling, backups, and SSL so you can focus on your product.

**Enterprise** is for high-volume businesses that need dedicated infrastructure, compliance guarantees, and custom integrations.

## Usage-Based Pricing

Cloud pricing has two components: a **base fee** and **usage charges** based on your transaction volume.

### Base Fee

The $299/mo base fee covers managed hosting, auto-scaling, daily backups, SSL, and email support.

### Included Usage

Each Cloud plan includes generous usage allowances:

| Metric | Included | Overage |
|--------|----------|---------|
| Invoices generated | 10,000/mo | $0.02 per invoice |
| Active subscriptions | 5,000 | $0.05 per subscription |
| API requests | 600/min | Throttled (no overage charge) |
| Webhook deliveries | Unlimited | — |
| Customers | Unlimited | — |

<Info>
Usage is calculated monthly. Overage charges appear on your next invoice. You can monitor your usage from the Cloud dashboard.
</Info>

### Example

A SaaS company with 3,000 active subscriptions generating 8,000 invoices/month pays **$299/mo** — well within the included allowances. At 15,000 invoices and 7,000 subscriptions, the total would be $299 + (5,000 x $0.02) + (2,000 x $0.05) = **$499/mo**.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I migrate from self-hosted to Cloud?">
    Yes. We provide a migration tool that exports your self-hosted data (plans, customers, subscriptions, invoices) and imports it into your Cloud instance. Contact [cloud@recurso.dev](mailto:cloud@recurso.dev) and we'll walk you through the process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my rate limit?">
    Requests over the limit receive a `429 Too Many Requests` response with a `Retry-After` header. On the Cloud plan, you can request a temporary or permanent rate limit increase by contacting support. Self-hosted users can configure their own rate limits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial for Cloud?">
    Cloud is currently in early access. Early access users receive a trial period — join the [waitlist](mailto:cloud@recurso.dev) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept for Cloud?">
    We accept credit/debit cards and bank transfers. Annual billing is available at a discount. Contact [cloud@recurso.dev](mailto:cloud@recurso.dev) for invoicing and payment options.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the open-source version really free?">
    Yes. Recurso is licensed under MIT. You can use, modify, and distribute it without paying anything. There are no premium features locked behind a paywall — every feature is available in the open-source edition.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I export my data from Cloud to self-host?">
    Yes. You own your data. We provide full database exports in standard formats so you can migrate to a self-hosted instance at any time. There is no lock-in.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does usage-based pricing work on Cloud?">
    Your $299/mo base fee includes 10,000 invoices and 5,000 active subscriptions per month. If you go over, you pay $0.02 per additional invoice and $0.05 per additional active subscription. Usage is calculated monthly and overage charges appear on your next invoice. API requests are rate-limited but do not incur overage charges.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why are add-on features free in open source but paid on Cloud?">
    The open-source edition includes everything because you provide the infrastructure to run it. On Cloud, add-ons like churn prediction and advanced analytics require additional compute resources that we manage on your behalf. The add-on pricing covers that infrastructure cost.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
