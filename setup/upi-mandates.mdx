## Overview

A **UPI AutoPay mandate** is the customer's standing authorization to auto-debit
recurring charges in India. It's created, authorized by the customer in their UPI
app, linked to a subscription, and then debited each cycle — with RBI-required
pre-debit notifications.

<Info>
Full lifecycle, frequencies, and webhooks: [UPI Mandates](/compliance/upi-mandates).
Requires Razorpay — see [payment gateways](/setup/payment-gateway).
</Info>

## Step 1: Create the mandate

Create a mandate for the customer with the amount cap and frequency. In the
dashboard, this happens on the subscription; or via the API:

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.recurso.dev/v1/mandates \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "customer_id": "cus_...",
    "max_amount": 500000,
    "frequency": "monthly"
  }'
```

## Step 2: Get the customer to authorize

The mandate returns an **authorization link/flow** — the customer approves it in
their UPI app. Until they authorize, the mandate is pending and can't be debited.

## Step 3: Link it to a subscription

Attach the authorized mandate to the subscription so each cycle's invoice is
auto-debited. Recurso sends the **pre-debit notification** ahead of each charge as
RBI requires.

## Verify your setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="The mandate authorizes">
    After the customer approves, the mandate status moves to active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="A cycle auto-debits">
    The subscription's next invoice is collected via the mandate, after the
    pre-debit notice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Revocation stops debits">
    Revoking the mandate stops further auto-debits.
  </Step>
</Steps>
