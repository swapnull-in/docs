## Overview

An organization groups related customers under a parent — useful for a company
with several billing entities, departments, or subsidiaries you want to see and
manage together.

<Info>
Concept + object detail: [Organizations](/advanced/organizations).
</Info>

## Step 1: Create the organization

In the dashboard (or API), create the organization:

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.recurso.dev/v1/organizations \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "name": "Acme Group" }'
```

## Step 2: Attach customers

Link customers to the organization so their subscriptions and invoices roll up
under the parent. A customer belongs to one organization.

## Step 3: View the hierarchy

Use the organization view to see all member customers and their billing together —
a consolidated picture of the account.

## Verify your setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="The organization exists">
    `GET /v1/organizations` lists it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customers roll up">
    Member customers appear under the organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>
