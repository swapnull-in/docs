## Overview

Churn monitoring scores each customer's risk of leaving from their billing signals
(failed payments, declining usage, downgrades) and surfaces the high-risk ones so
your team can reach out before it's too late.

<Info>
Dashboard detail: [Churn](/dashboard/churn).
</Info>

## Step 1: Let scoring run on your billing data

Churn risk is computed from activity already flowing through Recurso — no setup
beyond having live subscriptions, invoices, and payments. Each customer carries a
risk score that updates as their signals change.

## Step 2: Watch the high-risk list

In the dashboard, open **Churn** to see high-risk customers and churn alerts,
ranked by score. This is your intervention queue.

## Step 3: Act on the signal

Pair it with the tools that reduce churn: strong [dunning](/setup/dunning) for
involuntary churn, and [cancel flows with save offers](/setup/cancel-flows) for
voluntary churn.

## Verify your setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customers carry a risk score">
    Customer records show a churn-risk score/level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The high-risk list populates">
    The Churn dashboard ranks at-risk customers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Signals move the score">
    A failed payment or downgrade raises the affected customer's risk.
  </Step>
</Steps>
