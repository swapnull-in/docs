---

## Overview

Nexus tells you *where you must collect*; **registrations** record *where you
actually can*. Recording your registration number per state lets Recurso flag the
compliance gap that matters — a state where you have nexus but **no registration
on file** — and the **liability report** gives you the per-state figures to file.

<Info>
Assumes US sales tax and nexus are already configured — see
[Set up US sales tax end-to-end](/tutorials/us-sales-tax-setup).
</Info>

## Step 1: Record your registrations

For each state where you hold (or have applied for) a permit, record the
registration number and status. In the dashboard, go to **Settings → Tax nexus →
Sales-tax registrations**; or call the API (full replacement, like nexus states):

```bash
curl -X PUT https://api.recurso.dev/v1/settings/tax/registrations \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "registrations": [
      { "state_code": "CA", "registration_number": "CA-123", "status": "registered" },
      { "state_code": "NY", "status": "pending" }
    ]
  }'
```

Status is `registered` (permit in hand), `pending` (applied, awaiting), or
`not_registered`.

## Step 2: Close the nexus-but-not-registered gaps

On the US sales-tax page, Recurso compares your **nexus** states against your
**registrations** and banners any state where you have nexus but no active
registration — *"You have nexus but no registration on file in CA, NY."* Register
with each flagged state, then add the number here.

## Step 3: Pull the liability report

The report gives per-state figures for a filing period — gross sales, the
**taxable / exempt / non-taxable** split, tax collected, and whether you have
nexus there. In the dashboard it's the **Sales-tax liability** section (with a
year selector and totals); or via the API:

```bash
# Current year (default), or ?year=YYYY, or ?from=YYYY-MM-DD&to=YYYY-MM-DD
curl "https://api.recurso.dev/v1/settings/tax/liability?year=2026" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $API_KEY"
```

The three sale buckets **partition gross**: taxable (tax collected), exempt (a
[customer exemption](/setup/us-exemptions) applied), and non-taxable (no-nexus or
below-threshold). A state collecting tax without declared nexus is surfaced.

<Warning>
The report figures are exact, but confirm them with a tax professional before
filing — and note the built-in nexus threshold data is a monitoring aid until it
passes professional review.
</Warning>

## Verify your setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Registrations read back">
    `GET /v1/settings/tax/registrations` returns your states with their numbers
    and statuses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gaps are flagged">
    A state with nexus but no registration shows the gap banner on the US
    sales-tax page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The liability report ties out">
    Per state, taxable + exempt + non-taxable equals gross sales, and tax
    collected matches what you invoiced.
  </Step>
</Steps>
