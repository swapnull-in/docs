## Overview

A coupon is a reusable discount you apply to a subscription: **percentage** or
**fixed amount**, applied **once**, for a **number of periods**, or **forever**,
with optional restrictions (specific plans, a minimum amount, new customers only).

<Info>
Full option reference: [Coupons](/advanced/coupons).
</Info>

## Step 1: Create the coupon

In the dashboard, go to **Coupons → Create**; or via the API:

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.recurso.dev/v1/coupons \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "code": "LAUNCH20",
    "discount_type": "percent",
    "discount_value": 20,
    "duration": "repeating",
    "duration_in_periods": 3
  }'
```

- `discount_type` is `percent` or `amount` (fixed, in minor units).
- `duration` is `once`, `repeating` (with `duration_in_periods`), or `forever`.

## Step 2: Add restrictions (optional)

Limit where the coupon applies — specific plans, a minimum invoice amount, or
new customers only — so a promo can't be used outside its intent.

## Step 3: Apply it to a subscription

Attach the coupon at subscription creation or to an existing subscription. The
discount is applied on the next invoice and tracked as a redemption.

## Verify your setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="The coupon validates">
    `GET /v1/coupons/{code}` (or the validate endpoint) returns it as active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The discount lands on the invoice">
    A subscription with the coupon shows the reduced total on its next invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redemption is tracked">
    The coupon's redemption count increments; duration limits are respected.
  </Step>
</Steps>
