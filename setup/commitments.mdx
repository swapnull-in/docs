title: "Set Up Minimum Commitments"
description: "Bill a customer a minimum amount per period regardless of usage, with automatic true-up."
---

## Overview

A minimum commitment guarantees a floor of revenue per period: if a customer's
usage-based charges fall below the committed amount, Recurso **true-ups** the
difference on the invoice. Above it, they simply pay for what they used.

<Info>
Concept detail: [Commitments](/advanced/commitments). Pairs with
[usage-based billing](/tutorials/usage-based-saas).
</Info>

## Step 1: Set the commitment on the subscription

In the dashboard, open the subscription's billing panel and set **Commitment**
(the per-period minimum, in your currency); or via the API:

```bash
curl -X PUT https://api.recurso.dev/v1/subscriptions/{id}/commitment \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "amount": 100000 }'   # minor units; 0 clears the commitment
```

## Step 2: Let the true-up run at period close

At the end of each period, if metered + fixed charges are below the commitment, a
**true-up line** brings the invoice up to the committed minimum. Above it, no
true-up is added.

## Verify your setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="The commitment is set">
    The subscription shows its per-period minimum.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Low usage trues up">
    A period with usage below the commitment invoices at the committed amount, with
    a true-up line.
  </Step>
  <Step title="High usage doesn't">
    A period above the commitment bills actual usage, no true-up.
  </Step>
</Steps>
