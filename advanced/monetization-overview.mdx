## Overview

Flat monthly subscriptions are only the starting point. Monetization is the set of tools that let you shape pricing to how your product is actually used and sold — meter consumption, gate features by plan, run promotions, close larger deals with quotes, and issue adjustments after the fact. These building blocks combine so you can capture revenue the way your business grows.

## Key capabilities

- **Usage-based billing** — meter events and invoice consumption at the end of each cycle
- **Entitlements** — attach feature grants to plans and gate access with a fast check endpoint
- **Promotions** — percentage and fixed-amount coupons with redemption rules
- **Sales-led motion** — build, send, and convert quotes into subscriptions
- **Adjustments** — issue credit notes and add one-off charges or advance invoices
