## Overview

Recurso is built to sit at the center of your billing stack and talk to everything around it. This section covers the integration and platform surfaces: real-time **webhooks** for events, **accounting** sync, **payment gateway** configuration, multi-entity **organizations**, **virtual accounts** for bank collections, plus the **developer settings** and **idempotency** guarantees that make integrations safe to build against.

## Key capabilities

- **Webhooks** — real-time notifications for subscription, payment, and invoice events
- **Accounting sync** — connect your books and push billing data downstream
- **Payment gateways** — configure Razorpay and Stripe with currency-aware routing
- **Multi-entity** — model parent/child organizations and consolidated reporting
- **Bank collections** — issue virtual accounts for inbound transfers
- **Safe integrations** — API keys, developer settings, and idempotent request handling
