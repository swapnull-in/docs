## At a glance

| Category | Integration | Status | Configure with |
|---|---|---|---|
| Payments | Stripe | Stable | Dashboard (bring your own) or `STRIPE_SECRET_KEY` |
| Payments | Razorpay + UPI AutoPay | Stable | Dashboard (bring your own) or `RAZORPAY_KEY_ID`/`_SECRET` |
| Payments | GoCardless (SEPA/BACS bank debit) | Experimental | `GOCARDLESS_ACCESS_TOKEN` + [currency overrides](/advanced/payment-gateways#gocardless--adyen-experimental) |
| Payments | Adyen (cards/wallets) | Experimental | `ADYEN_API_KEY`, `ADYEN_MERCHANT_ACCOUNT` |
| Accounting | QuickBooks Online | Stable | OAuth from the dashboard |
| Accounting | Xero | Stable | OAuth from the dashboard |
| Accounting | Tally (local export) | Stable | built in |
| Accounting | NetSuite (SuiteTalk REST) | Experimental | connect provider `netsuite` |
| Tax | GST / e-Invoicing (IRN) | Stable | built in ([guide](/compliance/gst-returns)) |
| Tax | TaxJar (US sales tax) | Stable | `TAXJAR_API_KEY` |
| Tax | Avalara AvaTax | Experimental | `AVALARA_ACCOUNT_ID`/`_LICENSE_KEY` |
| Tax | VIES (EU VAT validation) | Stable | `VIES_ENABLED=true` |
| CRM | HubSpot (contact sync) | Experimental | `HUBSPOT_ACCESS_TOKEN` |
| Data | S3 / MinIO / R2 GL export | Experimental | `S3_EXPORT_BUCKET` (+ region/keys) |
| Automation | Zapier, n8n, Make | Stable | [signed webhooks](/advanced/automation) |
| Auth | Okta / Azure AD / any SAML IdP | Stable | [SSO guide](/advanced/okta-sso) |

**Experimental** means the adapter is built and unit-verified against the
provider's documented API but not yet certified against a live sandbox
account. Experimental integrations log their status at boot.

**Per-workspace vs operator-level.** Payment gateways, accounting, SSO, and the
GST/e-invoicing config are **per-workspace** — connect them from the dashboard
([Integrations](/dashboard/integrations), [Settings](/dashboard/settings),
[Team & security](/dashboard/team-security)). Each tenant can bring their own
Stripe/Razorpay account. Tax providers (TaxJar/Avalara), CRM (HubSpot), storage
(S3/MinIO), and the experimental gateways (GoCardless/Adyen) are **operator-level**
— set once for the whole deployment via the environment variables above.

## Residency guarantees

Under [`RESIDENCY_MODE=self_hosted`](/compliance/data-residency), SaaS
egress — QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, TaxJar, Avalara, HubSpot — is hard
disabled at construction. Payment gateways, your SMTP server, your S3
bucket, and your webhook endpoints remain available: they are
operator-configured destinations, not third-party data sharing.
