## Overview

**Disputes** (in the left nav) is the queue of invoice queries your customers
raise from the [customer portal](/dashboard/customer-portal). Each dispute ties
a customer's objection to a specific invoice, and your team resolves it with an
optional note.

<Info>
Disputes here are **customer-raised invoice queries**. Resolving a dispute is a
triage action on the record — it doesn't itself move money. Use a
[credit note](/dashboard/credit-notes) or refund if an adjustment is warranted.
</Info>

## The disputes list

The table shows one row per dispute:

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| Invoice | The disputed invoice (short id) |
| Customer | Who raised it (short id) |
| Reason | The customer's stated reason |
| Status | **open** (amber) or **resolved** (green), with the resolution note if any |
| Raised | When the dispute was created |

Use the **status filter** in the toolbar to switch between **Open**,
**Resolved**, and **All**. The page opens filtered to **Open** so the
outstanding queue is front and center.

## Resolve a dispute

Open disputes show a **Resolve** button.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Resolve">
    A dialog opens showing the invoice and the customer's reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a resolution note (optional)">
    Record what was done about it — the note is **visible to your team** and
    shows against the resolved dispute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Mark resolved">
    The dispute moves to **resolved** and drops out of the open queue.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Resolving only records the outcome. If the customer is owed money or an invoice
correction, issue a [credit note](/dashboard/credit-notes) or refund
separately.
</Warning>
