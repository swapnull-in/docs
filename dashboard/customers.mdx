## Overview

**Customers** (in the left nav) is the directory of everyone you bill. It shows
a searchable, paginated table and opens a detail panel for each customer where
you can edit or archive them.

## The customer list

Each row shows:

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Customer** | Name and email. |
| **Status** | **Active** (has active subscriptions), **Inactive** (none), or **Archived**. |
| **Risk** | The churn-risk score with a **Low / Medium / High** label. |
| **Subscriptions** | Count of active subscriptions. |
| **Joined** | When the customer was created. |
| (row end) | A **copy portal link** button. |

<Info>
The **Risk** badge is colored by score: green (Low, under 20), amber (Medium,
20–49), and red (High, 50+). It only appears once a churn score has been
calculated.
</Info>

### Search and filter

- **Search** by name or email — the box debounces, so results update shortly
  after you stop typing.
- A **status toggle** filters between **All**, **Active**, and **Inactive**.
- Paging is server-driven; use **Prev / Next** at the bottom (10 per page).

### Copy portal link

The link icon at the end of a row copies a **portal login link** prefilled with
the customer's email. The customer enters the self-service portal via a
magic-link login — see [Customer portal](/dashboard/customer-portal).

## Add a customer

Click **Add customer** (top right, or from the empty state). A right-side sheet
opens with two sections.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact information">
    **Customer name** and **Email** are required; **Phone** is optional. The
    form validates that the email is well-formed before it will submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Billing details">
    Enter a **Billing address**, then pick a **Country** (United States, India,
    Canada, or United Kingdom) and a **State / Province**.

    <Info>
    Choosing **India** switches the last two fields to **Place of supply
    (State)** — picked from a list of Indian states — and **GSTIN**, so tax is
    handled correctly for Indian customers. Other countries show a free-text
    **Tax ID / VAT Number**.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click **Create customer**. The new customer appears in the list
    immediately. **Cancel** closes the sheet without saving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Customer detail

Click any row to open the **Customer details** sheet. It shows the customer's
name, email, and badges for archived status and active-subscription count,
followed by grouped fields:

- **Contact** — email and phone.
- **Billing & tax** — billing address, tax type, GSTIN, and place of supply
  (shown only when there's something to display).
- **Record** — customer ID, joined date, churn risk, active subscription count,
  and referral code if one exists.

### Edit a customer

Click **Edit customer** to switch the panel into an inline form. You can update
name, email, phone, GSTIN, place of supply, and the full billing address (line,
city, state, ZIP, and ISO-2 country). Click **Save customer** to apply, or
**Cancel** to discard. Email cannot be left blank.

### Archive / restore

Click **Archive** to remove a customer from active use. A confirmation dialog
explains what happens.

<Warning>
Archiving is **blocked while the customer has active subscriptions** — cancel
or move those first. Billing history is always kept, and an archived customer
can be **restored** at any time (the button becomes **Restore**).
</Warning>
