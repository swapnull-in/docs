## Overview

The Recurso dashboard at [app.recurso.dev](https://app.recurso.dev) is the
operator console for your billing: catalog, customers, money movement, revenue
reporting, and settings. Every screen is backed by the same API the
[SDKs](/sdk) use, so anything you do here you can also automate.

This guide documents **every section** of the dashboard — what it shows, the
actions you can take, and how it connects to the rest of the product — plus
task-oriented **workflows** that span several sections.

<Info>
New here? Start with the [Overview](/dashboard/overview) for the home screen,
then follow [Onboard your first customer](/dashboard/workflows/first-subscription)
to bill someone end to end.
</Info>

## Roles & permissions

Some actions are limited to **owners and admins** (connecting a gateway,
managing the team, changing settings). Members can view and operate day to day.
Where a section is role-gated, its guide says so.
