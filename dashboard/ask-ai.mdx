## Overview

**Ask AI** (the *Ask your data* page, at `/ask`) lets you ask billing questions in
plain English. Recurso translates your question into a read-only, tenant-scoped
query, runs it against your own data, and shows the result — so every answer is
grounded in **your** workspace, never anyone else's.

<Info>
Answers are read-only. Ask AI only reads your billing data to answer a question;
it never changes anything.
</Info>

## Asking a question

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type your question">
    Enter a question in the box — for example *"Which customers churned last
    month?"* — and click **Ask**. While it works, the button reads **Thinking…**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Or pick an example">
    Chips below the box offer starter questions you can click to run immediately,
    such as:

    - *What was my MRR growth over the last 3 months?*
    - *Which plan has the most active subscriptions?*
    - *How many invoices are overdue, and for how much?*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the result">
    Row-shaped answers render as a table; single values render as text. Expand
    **Show the SQL it ran** to see the exact query Recurso executed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notes

- Questions run as **read-only, tenant-scoped** queries — results only ever cover
  your own account's data.
- If a question can't be answered, you'll be asked to rephrase it.
- Ask AI depends on a GenAI provider being configured for the deployment. If it
  isn't enabled, the page tells you so instead of returning a result.
