## Overview

**Wallets** (in the left nav) holds **prepaid balances** for your customers. A
wallet is money a customer has already paid in — Recurso spends it down before
it reaches for credit notes or charges the payment gateway, so a customer with
a funded wallet is billed against that balance first.

Each wallet belongs to one customer and is denominated in a single currency.
The list shows every wallet with its owner, current **balance**, and whether
**auto-recharge** is on.

<Info>
Wallets are drained **before credit notes and the payment gateway**. This makes
them useful for prepaid credit, promotional grants, and top-up-style billing.
</Info>

## The wallet list

The table has a row per wallet:

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| Customer | The wallet owner |
| Balance | Current spendable balance, in the wallet's currency |
| Auto-recharge | A green badge (`below X → +Y`) when enabled, otherwise **off** |

Search filters by customer or currency. Click any row to open its
**transaction history**.

## Create a wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create wallet">
    Opens a side panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick the customer and currency">
    Choose the customer from the picker and set a 3-letter currency code
    (defaults to `INR`). Click **Create wallet**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The new wallet starts at a zero balance — fund it with a top-up.

## Top up a balance

Click **Top up** on a wallet row to add funds.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the amount">
    In the wallet's currency (for example `5000.00`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a source">
    - **Manual (money received)** — you received the money out of band and are
      recording it.
    - **Promotional credit** — a grant you're giving the customer.

    Click **Top up**. The new balance is available immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Auto-recharge

Auto-recharge keeps a wallet from running dry. Click **Auto-recharge** on a row
to configure it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set a threshold and a recharge amount">
    When the balance falls **below the threshold**, the wallet is automatically
    topped up by the **recharge amount** using the customer's saved payment
    method. Both values are in the wallet's currency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save or disable">
    Click **Save** to enable it. To turn it off later, reopen the dialog and
    click **Disable**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Auto-recharge needs both a threshold **and** a recharge amount — you can't set
one without the other. Disabling clears both.
</Warning>

## Transaction history

Click a wallet row to see its recent movements (newest first). Each entry is
tagged by **type**:

| Type | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `top_up` | Money added (manual or promotional), shown as `+` |
| `drain` | Balance spent against billing, shown as `−` |

Each row shows the amount, the source (where relevant), and the resulting
**balance after** the movement, so you can trace the running balance.
