## Overview

Recurso opens on **Home** — the landing screen at the root of the dashboard.
It answers two questions fast: *what needs my attention today* and *how is
billing trending*. A deeper, board-grade view lives one click away under
**Executive Summary**.

<Info>
Every tile and strip on these screens is a link. Click a KPI or an alert to
jump straight to the section it summarizes.
</Info>

## Home

The header carries quick-create buttons for **Customer**, **Subscription**, and
**Plan** — each opens the matching create flow.

### Needs attention

A strip below the header surfaces anything that needs action right now, built
from live data:

- **Overdue invoices** — count and the total past due (per currency), links to
  [invoice aging](/dashboard/invoices).
- **Open disputes** — chargebacks waiting on you, links to Disputes.
- **Churn alerts** — customers whose risk score spiked, links to Churn risk.

When all three are clear, the strip collapses to a single green **All clear**
line.

### KPI row

Four stat cards summarize the account:

| Card | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **MRR** | Monthly recurring revenue. Opens the [Executive Summary](/dashboard/overview). |
| **Active Subscriptions** | Count of subscriptions currently `active`. |
| **Churn** | Canceled vs. total subscriptions, as a percentage. |
| **Recovered Revenue** | Amount recovered via smart dunning, in your reporting currency. |

### Charts and recent activity

- **Revenue over time** — an area chart of invoiced revenue over the trailing
  90 days. Each currency gets its own line (amounts in different currencies are
  never summed without FX).
- **Recent invoices** — the eight newest invoices with customer, amount, and a
  status badge. Click a row to open that invoice's detail sheet; **View all**
  goes to [Invoices](/dashboard/invoices).

## Executive Summary

Open **Executive Summary** (the MRR card on Home links here) for a fuller
revenue read-out. A period toggle in the header — **30d / 60d / 90d** — sets
the window for the movement and retention figures.

<Info>
Each metric is fetched independently, so if one source is unavailable that
single tile shows a dash (`—`) rather than breaking the whole page.
</Info>

The screen is grouped into sections of stat cards, plus two charts:

### Revenue

| Card | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **MRR** | Monthly recurring revenue (normalized). |
| **ARR** | Annual run-rate. |
| **Net change** | Ending vs. starting MRR over the selected window; turns red if negative. |
| **Net dollar retention** | Trailing-window NDR; needs MRR history, flagged if under 100%. |

### Charts

- **MRR trend** — end-of-month MRR for the last six months (appears once you
  have at least two months of history).
- **MRR movement** — what grew and shrank in the selected window.

### MRR movement

**New**, **Expansion**, **Contraction**, and **Churned** for the window.
Churned turns red when cancellations outpace new plus expansion.

### Unit economics

**ARPA** (per account), **ARPU** (per subscription), **LTV** (at current
churn, needs history), and **Active customers** with a subscription count.

### Cash & recognition

**Outstanding** and **Overdue** receivables, **Deferred revenue**, and
**Recognized (MTD)**. Overdue is flagged amber or red as it grows as a share of
outstanding.

Every card links to the matching finance report so you can drill in.
