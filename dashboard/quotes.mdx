## Overview

**Quotes** (in the left nav) are the sales-side of billing: a priced,
line-itemed proposal you send to a customer, that converts into an invoice once
accepted. Each quote moves through a lifecycle you can see at a glance:

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Draft** | Being prepared; not yet sent |
| **Sent** | Delivered to the customer |
| **Accepted** | Customer agreed — ready to convert |
| **Declined** | Customer rejected it |
| **Expired** | Passed its *valid until* date |

The list shows the **Quote** number, **Customer**, **Amount** (the total),
**Status**, and **Created** date. Search by text and filter by status. Each row
has contextual actions on the right.

## Create a quote

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open New quote">
    Click **New quote** to open the side panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set quote details">
    - **Customer** (required) — pick from your customers.
    - **Currency** — USD, EUR, GBP, or INR.
    - **Valid until** — an optional expiry date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add line items">
    Each line item has a **Description**, **Qty**, and **Unit price**. The
    **Amount** column computes automatically. Use **Add item** to add rows and the
    trash icon to remove them (at least one line item is required).

    <Info>
    Unit price and the **Tax** and **Discount** fields are entered in **minor
    units** (cents) — the field labels show `(¢)`. The panel live-updates the
    **Subtotal**, **Tax**, **Discount**, and **Total** as you type.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes & terms">
    **Notes** are visible to the customer. **Terms & conditions** default to
    standard payment terms and can be edited. Click **Create quote** — it starts
    as a **draft**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Working a quote

Row actions change with the quote's status:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send">
    A **draft** shows a **Send** action. Sending moves the quote to **sent**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Convert to invoice">
    An **accepted** quote that hasn't been converted yet shows a **Convert to
    invoice** action. Converting turns the quote into a live invoice so billing
    can begin; once converted, the action disappears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View details">
    The **⋯** action (or clicking the quote number) opens the detail panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The quote detail panel

The detail sheet leads with the **Total amount**, then lists every **line item**
with its quantity and unit price, followed by a **Subtotal / Discount / Tax /
Total** breakdown. Below that it shows the **Customer**, **Valid until**,
**Created** date, and any **Notes** and **Terms** you entered.
