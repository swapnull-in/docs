## Overview

**Organizations** (in the left nav) is the multi-entity view. An organization
is a grouping that sits *above* individual tenants (workspaces): you attach
several tenants to it, and Recurso rolls their recurring revenue up into a
single **consolidated MRR** figure. Tenants themselves keep their own data —
the organization is just the umbrella.

Use it when one company runs several billing tenants (brands, regions,
subsidiaries) and finance wants one combined number.

<Info>
An organization records a **name** and an **owner email**. Adding a tenant to a
group does not move or merge any of that tenant's billing data.
</Info>

## The organizations list

The main screen is a table of every organization you've created:

| Column | Shows |
| --- | --- |
| Organization | The organization's name |
| Owner | The owner email, or `—` if none |
| ID | The short (first 8 characters) organization ID |

Click any row to open its detail panel. If you have none yet, the empty state
prompts you to create one.

## Create an organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New organization">
    The button is in the page header (and in the empty state).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a name and owner email">
    Both fields are required — for example **Acme Group** and
    `owner@example.com`. Click **Create organization**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage a group

Clicking a row opens a right-side panel with everything about that
organization.

### Consolidated MRR

When available, the panel leads with a **Consolidated MRR** card — the combined
monthly recurring revenue across all attached tenants, in the group's reporting
currency.

### Tenants

Below that is the **Tenants** list. Each attached tenant shows its name (or
short ID) and its full tenant UUID. From here you can:

- **Add a tenant** — paste the tenant's UUID into the field and click **Add**.
  The consolidated MRR and list refresh once it's attached.
- **Remove a tenant** — click the trash icon on its row. This only detaches it
  from the group; the tenant and its data are untouched.

### Rename

Click the pencil next to the organization's name, edit it inline, and click
**Save**.

### Delete

**Delete organization** (at the bottom of the panel) removes the grouping only.

<Warning>
Deleting an organization removes the umbrella and its consolidated view.
The tenants that belonged to it are **not** deleted — they continue to bill
independently.
</Warning>
