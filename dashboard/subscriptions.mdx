## Overview

**Subscriptions** (in the left nav) lists every recurring subscription and is
where you create new ones, change plans, pause or cancel, and attach add-ons.

## The subscription list

Each row shows:

| Column | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Customer** | Name and email. |
| **Status** | A colored badge — see below. |
| **Plan** | The plan name. |
| **Amount** | The plan price and interval (e.g. `/mo`, `/yr`). |
| **Start date** | Start of the current period. |
| **Next invoice** | End of the current period (when the next invoice generates). |

### Status badges

| Badge | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Active** | Billing normally. |
| **Trialing** | In a trial period. |
| **Paused** | Billing temporarily stopped. |
| **Past due** | A payment failed and is being recovered. |
| **Canceled** | Ended; no further invoices. |

### Search and filter

- **Search** by customer name, email, or ID (debounced).
- Filter by **Status**, by **Plan**, and by **Date** (all time / last 30 days /
  this month).
- Paging is server-driven with **Prev / Next** (10 per page).

## Create a subscription

Click **Add subscription**. A right-side sheet opens.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer & plan">
    Pick an existing **Customer** and a **Plan** from the dropdowns. The plan
    dropdown shows each plan's price and interval.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scheduling & billing">
    - **Start date** — defaults to today.
    - **Billing anchor** — **Acquisition date** (billing repeats from the start
      date) or **Calendar billing (1st of month)** (the first period is
      prorated and all renewals align to the 1st).
    - **Payment terms** — **Due on receipt** (default), or **Net 15 / 30 / 45 /
      60** days after the invoice date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize and create">
    Tick the **billing authorization** consent box to confirm recurring billing
    is authorized — the form won't submit without it. A **Summary** box
    previews the customer, plan, start date, terms, and total. Click **Create
    subscription**.

    <Info>
    If the plan runs through Razorpay recurring billing, a Razorpay
    authorization dialog opens to capture the customer's mandate before the
    subscription becomes active.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Subscription detail

Click any row to open the detail sheet. The header shows the subscription ID
and a live **status** indicator. Below it a grid lists the customer, plan,
amount, created date, current period, and the upcoming invoice date and amount.

### Lifecycle actions

The action buttons shown depend on the current status; each opens a
confirmation dialog:

| Action | Available when | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Pause** | Active | Billing stops until resumed; access follows your pause policy. |
| **Resume** | Paused | Billing restarts from the current period. |
| **Cancel** | Active or paused | Ends the subscription; no further invoices. |
| **Reactivate** | Canceled | Restarts billing on the same plan from the next cycle. |

### Change plan

Click **Change plan**, pick a new plan, and Recurso fetches a **proration
preview**: credit for unused time, the prorated charge for the new plan, tax,
and the net **Due now** (or **Credited to account**), plus the next invoice
amount and date. Click **Confirm plan change** to apply.

### Add-ons

The **Add-ons** section lists any attached add-on plans and their quantity.
Pick an add-on plan, set a quantity, and click **Add** to attach one; the **×**
removes it.

<Info>
Add-ons **bill from the next invoice**, not immediately.
</Info>

### Advance invoice & commitment

For active subscriptions, two extra controls appear:

- **Advance invoice** — generate one invoice now covering the next N billing
  periods (1–60).
- **Commitment** — set a minimum amount billed per period regardless of usage;
  set it to 0 to clear.

### Timeline

A short timeline shows when the subscription was **created** and when the
**next renewal** is scheduled.
