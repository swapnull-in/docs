## Overview

**Cancel flows** (in the left nav, also called cancellation flows) let you
intercept a customer who's about to cancel and try to keep them. A flow is an
ordered sequence of steps — a survey, one or more retention offers, and a final
confirmation — shown to the customer during cancellation.

Each flow has a name, an **Active**/**Inactive** state, an optional **Default**
flag, and a **cooldown** that limits how often the same customer sees it.

<Info>
Cancel flows are the *save* half of retention: [Churn risk](/dashboard/churn)
tells you who's likely to leave; a cancel flow tries to save them at the moment
they act.
</Info>

## The flow list

Each flow shows as a card with its name, badges (**Default**, and
**Active**/**Inactive**), and its cooldown in days. Click **Configure steps** to
open the flow detail panel.

## Create a flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New flow">
    Opens a side panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name it and set a cooldown">
    - **Name** — e.g. `Standard retention flow`.
    - **Cooldown (days)** — the minimum days before the same customer sees this
      flow again (defaults to 30).
    - **Use as the default flow** — optional checkbox to make this the flow
      shown when no other applies.

    Click **Create flow** — you're taken straight into configuring its steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure steps

The flow panel controls the flow and its steps. At the top you can:

- **Activate / Deactivate** the flow.
- **Set as default** (or unset it).
- See the **cooldown** in days.

### Performance stats

When available, the panel shows three stats for the flow:

| Stat | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Completed** | Customers who went through the flow |
| **Saved** | Customers retained rather than canceled |
| **Save rate** | Saved ÷ completed |

### Step types

Steps are shown to the customer in order. Add a step with **Add step**, then
pick its **Order** and **Type**:

| Type | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Survey** | Asks why they're leaving — a set of reason options |
| **Offer** | Presents a retention offer (e.g. a discount), with a headline and one or more offers |
| **Confirmation** | The final confirm-cancellation step, with a message |

Each type has its own configuration below the type picker. Edit or delete steps
from the list; deleting a step doesn't affect sessions already in progress.

<Info>
A typical flow is **Survey → Offer → Confirmation**: understand the reason,
counter it with an offer, and only then confirm the cancellation.
</Info>
